Get Output's Arcade Sampler for Less with Discount Codes

Output's Arcade is a powerful and versatile virtual instrument plugin for music producers. With its vast library of loops, samples, and presets, Arcade makes it easy to find inspiration and add unique sounds to your tracks. An Overview of Output's Arcade Sampler

Released in 2018, Arcade is a sample playback instrument developed by Output. It comes with over 10,000 loops and one-shot samples expertly crafted and performed by professional musicians. Genres covered include EDM, hip hop, rock, pop, cinematic, and more.

The samples are organized into different "packs" with descriptive names like Analog Strings, Vintage Keys, and Modern RnB. You can mix and match sounds from different packs to create unique combinations. Filters, effects, and macros allow for deep sound design possibilities.

One of Arcade's standout features is the intelligent "Chord Packs". These contain loops and samples that automatically conform to the chords you play on your MIDI keyboard, making harmonic experimentation easy.

Other key features of Output's Arcade:

- Browser with search, tags, and favorites for finding sounds quickly
- Ability to drag and drop samples to your DAW or sampler
- Slice mode for chopping loops into individual hits
- Built-in pitch, filter, distortion, delay, and reverb effects
- Ability to create and export your own sample packs

With its versatile sound library and intuitive workflow, Arcade is popular among producers of all genres looking for studio-grade content.

Making the Most of Your Arcade Trial

To help you decide if Arcade is right for your productions, Output offers a free 14-day trial. When evaluating Arcade:

- Test it on finished tracks to hear how it blends with your other instruments
- Try building full melodies and chord progressions with the Chord Packs
- Design your own sounds using filters, effects, and custom sample packs
- Browse all content categories to get a feel for the scope of sounds
- Check CPU usage and plugin stability within your usual projects

Getting hands-on during the trial period will help you determine if Arcade's sounds and workflows are a fit for your creative process. That way, if you do decide to purchase it, you'll make the most of your investment.
